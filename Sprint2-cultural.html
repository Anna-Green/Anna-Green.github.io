Learning Goals

My Plans


Long Term Goals

Long term, my goals are that I want a career that I can be creative in, allows me to travel or work from anywhere and is in a stable industry. I am really interested in Artificial Intelligence and also Virtual Reality and gaming so I would eventually like to look into those fields in the future.



Soft Skills

I would like to have upskilled in things like, time-management, asking for help when needed and creating and sticking to routines and structure.



Strengths and Weaknesses

You can look at my strengths and weaknesses Here but in short terms, I am creative, fun, determined and positive. My weaknesses are sometimes I get discouraged, I do not always like asking for help and I can be distracted at times.



My Commitment and Routine

I am working on creating a routine where I work only between a certain set of hours (during foundations its 9am-5pm) so that I can enjoy a healthy life balance and not get overworked. In those hours I can have breaks, but I don’t look at my phone, never have tabs open that aren’t work related and turn of all notifications on all devices. I am reaching out and asking people for clarification if I don’t understand something or I’m having trouble. I know that my facilitator is there when I need to ask her something or if not, there is the Slack channel and my cohort!



The Facilitators and my future at DEV Academy

What I expect from the facilitation team is guided help. So, if I do have questions, instead of answering me straight away, they can provide resources to help me find the answer. I also would like to know if I am doing something wrong so that I can change it and correct my path. All in all, I hope for new experiences, projects and learning both from myself and the team at DEV Academy. In my first 2 weeks I have already learned so much more than I ever thought I would and cant wait to see what the rest of the course is like.
