Problem Solving Techniques and Processess


A problem Thatm I Came Across

A problem that I came across was when I created
and coded my blog on a copy rather than the original document that it was supposed to be on.
I was really confused as to why I could open it on my computer but not on the internet.
I had to go back and work through my entire progress to see where I went wrong and once I found my mistake,
it was easy to transfer all of my files from the copied document, onto the original.


So What Did I Learn From This?

I learned that I had to pay more attention to the instructions that I was given
and to go through all of my work throughout the process to make sure that Im where im meant to be.


Valuable Resources


I have learned that if I have questions, I can always ask my classmates on slack,
my facilitator during our meetings and that google is an invaluable resource.
